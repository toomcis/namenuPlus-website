how it works

Open. Search. Eat.

01
Open the app
No sign-up required. Guest mode works from day one. Your location sorts nearby restaurants automatically.
02
Browse or search
Scroll through today's menus or search a specific dish. Filter by price, allergens, or delivery.
03
Tap and go
Tap an address to open in Maps. Call the restaurant directly. Done.

privacy

We mean it when we say privacy-first.

✓ No ads, ever. Not in the app, not in the API.
✓ No account required. Guest mode works for everything.
✓ All data sharing is opt-in and clearly labelled.
✓ Fully open source. MIT licensed. Inspect everything.
✓ Self-hostable. Run your own backend, keep all data local.
✓ Delete your account and all your data anytime, instantly.
